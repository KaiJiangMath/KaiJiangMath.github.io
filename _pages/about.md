I am a Professor of Mathematics at Xiangtan University and a National High-Level Young Talent scholar. My work focuses on applied mathematics problems inspired by irrational numbers, and I lead projects including a National Key R&D Program subproject, National Natural Science Foundation grants, and provincial innovation programs.

My research interests include irrationality-inspired applied mathematics, computable modeling and simulation for soft-matter systems, and AI for Science.
