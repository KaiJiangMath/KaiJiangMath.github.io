## Selected Activities

- **Research Group Recruitment (ongoing)**: Long-term recruitment of postdoctoral researchers, PhD students, academic master's students in mathematics, professional master's students in applied statistics, and undergraduate students.

- **Major Project Leadership (ongoing)**: Leading and participating in national and provincial projects, including one National Key R&D Program topic, NSFC projects, and Hunan provincial talent projects.

- **Academic Service (ongoing)**: Editorial board member of *Mathematica Numerica Sinica (Computational Mathematics)*.

- **Teaching and Training (ongoing)**: Teaching core undergraduate and graduate courses in algebra, analysis, optimization, and scientific computation.

- **International Academic Exchange (2015-2017)**: Visiting researcher at the National University of Singapore.
