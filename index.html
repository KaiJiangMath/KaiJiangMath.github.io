Biography

I am a Professor at Xiangtan University. My work focuses on how irrational structures shape mathematical models and scientific computing, with applications in quasiperiodic systems, complex materials, and numerical algorithms. I currently lead projects including a National Key R&D topic and multiple NSFC grants.

Research Interests

  • Irrationality-driven applied mathematics
  • Computable modeling and simulation for soft-matter systems
  • AI for Science and scientific machine learning
